    Training Overview

    Welcome to the Beyond the Code (India Program). This portal contains all necessary information regarding curriculum, scheduling, and certification requirements.

    Training Tracks

    This program is conducted via two distinct tracks. Please review the requirements for each track below.

    1. Normal Track (4 Months)

    This track covers the comprehensive curriculum from foundational functional programming to advanced cognitive architectures.

    Access: Online tutorials, training materials, and meeting recordings are open to the entire community.

    Requirement: Trainees must propose a team project one month prior to the conclusion of the training.

    Certification: Successful completion of the proposed project is required to be certified.

    1. Fast Track (2 Months)

    Designed for individuals who have independently studied the provided materials and possess a strong grasp of the core concepts.

    Selection Process: Candidates must pass a preliminary team hacking session to demonstrate proficiency.

    Structure: Includes a rapid revision of core concepts followed by an intensive project phase.

    Requirement: Trainees form teams after the first month to begin work on the capstone project.

    Attendance & Retention Policy

    We maintain high standards for participation to ensure the quality of the cohort.

    Mandatory Attendance

    Attendance is required for all sessions. Any trainee with more than three absences will be removed from the active training cohort.

    Community Replacement Mechanism

    Open spots resulting from trainee removal will be filled by members of the wider community on a monthly basis.

    Selection Method: Candidates are selected via MeTTa coding challenges based on content covered to date.

    Criteria: Nominations are awarded to the fastest correct submissions, subject to available capacity.
